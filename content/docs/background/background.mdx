## CSS Grid Background

A full-width wrapper that renders a subtle CSS grid pattern behind your content. The grid lines automatically adapt between light and dark themes, keeping your foreground content clearly visible through layered z-index stacking. Perfect for hero sections, landing pages, or any area that needs visual texture without distracting from the main content.
